Caldwell Velnambi

Founder & Chairman

Caldwell Velnambi, the founder and chairman of Nobl Q, has had a remarkable 30-year career marked by groundbreaking contributions, particularly in enterprise resource planning. Under his guidance, Nobl Q has witnessed remarkable growth in the ERP sector, with consulting and implementation being the main focus, and has achieved significant milestones to becoming Gold partners with SAP and Acumatica.

Caldwell has shaped the trajectory of Nobl Q and played a pivotal role in establishing Nobl Q as a key player in the market. With a career defined by foresight, customer experience and resilience, he has been at the forefront of the company’s evolution. Caldwell’s track record is a testament to his ability to transform challenges into opportunities, a quality that continues to drive Nobl Q’s success story. Caldwell remains committed to Nobl Q’s continued growth by driving inorganic initiatives, ventures, and collaborations. Besides being an entrepreneurial leader, Caldwell contributes extensively to his cultural roots and is deeply involved in the Federation of Tamil Sangams of North America (FeTNA). His commitment to giving back to the local community aligns with our broader mission of prioritizing people, celebrating diversity, and making a meaningful impact. He resides in Coppell, Texas, with his family.

Nepoleon Duraisamy

Co-Chairman

Nepoleon Duraisamy has played a pivotal role as the founder and Chairman of Jeevan Technologies. Awakened by the large-scale unemployment of qualified youngsters, he forayed into IT Entrepreneurship and launched Jeevan Technologies IT operations in Chennai, India in the year 2000 and guided the company to become a globally trusted technology transformation partner for enterprises around the world. His entrepreneurial acumen and vision have been instrumental in shaping Jeevan Technologies into a distinguished entity in the global business landscape. Nepoleon holds the position of Co – Chairman at Nobl Q

In addition to his success in the business world, Nepoleon has showcased his versatility as a distinguished actor in the Tamil movie industry in India. Furthermore, he served as a Member of the Legislative Assembly in Tamil Nadu, India, from 2001 to 2006, demonstrating his commitment to public service. His leadership skills were further recognized when he served as the Central Minister for Social Justice and Empowerment in India until March 2013. One of Nepoleon’s notable social entrepreneurial endeavors includes the establishment of the Myopathy unit under Jeevan Foundations. This initiative is dedicated to supporting underprivileged children affected by Muscular Dystrophy, showcasing his commitment to making a positive impact on society. Nepoleon currently resides in Nashville, TN, with his family since 2011.

Jayasudha Nepoleon

Member, Board of Directors

As a founding member of Jeevan Technologies, Jayasudha Nepoleon has been a strong pillar in driving the organization to unprecedented heights and currently holds the position of Board Member at Nobl Q. Her transformative leadership style, characterized by a commitment to innovation and excellence is a great asset to Nobl Q.

Her academic background with a master’s in business management and a specialization in finance has contributed a wealth of financial acumen to her previous roles in Jeevan and has laid the foundation for her positions as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) and Global President of Jeevan before assuming the responsibilities of Nobl Q.

Prabhu Antony

Member, Board of Directors

Prabhu Antony is a member of the board of directors at Nobl Q. He is a seasoned investment professional who has delivered strategic guidance to IT Services and SaaS firms. His proficiency spans corporate development, public listings, mergers, and acquisitions, as well as private equity transactions.

He is currently the co-Founder and President of Stonebridge (Nasdaq: APAC) a publicly traded SPAC that raised $200M in an IPO and currently reverse merging with Southeast Asian Fintech-As-a-Service leader – Digi Asia, a Mastercard and Leapfrog Investments portfolio company. He is also the co-founder of Sett & Lucas, a Hong Kong headquartered investment bank focused on middle market buyout of Technology Services and SaaS companies, and High Circle – a neo bank for HNWIs. He also serves as the Global Chief Investment Officer at Scieniti, a UK headquartered family office with control and minority investments across 11 industries, employing over 1000 people in 30 countries with over $500M AUM. In his capacity as a highly engaged entrepreneur, Prabhu assumes the roles of an investor, board member, advisor, and observer for numerous enterprises spanning the United States, the United Kingdom, and Asia. His involvement encompasses a broad spectrum of industries, including Technology, Healthcare, Human Capital Management, Financial Services, Media and Entertainment, Real Estate, Hospitality, Education, and Manufacturing. He is an alumnus of the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania and Anna University in India.

Karthik Ramamoorthy

Member, Board of Directors

Karthik Ramamoorthy, GM and SVP of Products at Zuora Inc, a leading cloud-based subscription management and billing solutions provider, brings over 20 years of expertise in finance, revenue management, AI initiatives, and product leadership. Previously leading Zuora's professional services team, he played a pivotal role in delivering exceptional value to clients. A customer-centric and outcome-driven leader, Karthik is actively involved in the startup ecosystem, where he advises and backs exceptional founders building software for the enterprise. His passion for fostering innovation and supporting emerging entrepreneurs demonstrates his commitment to driving positive change within the industry.

Karthik's diverse background positions him as a thought leader at the intersection of technology and finance. As a Board member, he is committed to leveraging his multifaceted experience for strategic decision-making, innovation, and ensuring long-term organizational success. Karthik holds a master’s degree in computer science from New Mexico Tech University and a B.E. in Computer Science from Madras University.

Suresh Periasami

Chief Delivery Officer

Suresh Periasami is the Chief Delivery Officer of Nobl Q and is focused on client delight, execution excellence, building world-class delivery teams, and delivering industry-leading profitable growth. He is a founding member of Jeevan Technologies (2000), Think trade Inc (2005), and Jeevan IT Academy (2009) and has played a pivotal role in steering these ventures towards success, particularly in the development of cutting-edge products for various industry verticals.
